ABOUT

My name is David Valencia, I am a Telematic Engineer, certified Project Manager and Digital Presence enthusiast born in México City. My current professional interests boil down to three fields:

01.

Telecommunications

Since I was a teenager, I have felt deeply interested in all things telecom, its latest trends and technologies, such as 5G, IoT, SDN, NFV, Smart Cities and applied topics such as Information Security, and Data Science. I have been in the vendor, client (AT&T) and consultancy firm ecosystems, which give me a 360° view of the industry needs.

02.

Project Management

During my time as Integration Engineer for a major telecommunications vendor, I wondered who was the mastermind behind project plans and execution. Thus I started my path to become part of the international PMP community, while I matured my leadership and client-facing skills with inter-cultural teams and partners.

03.

Consulting

Later on, I joined an innovative boutique consultancy firm dedicated to the telco industry. There I grew and galvanized an interdisciplinary career, held together by an elevated understanding of business strategies behind telecom. Thanks to this group, I transformed into a trusted partner to the C-Suite for several organizations.
